Tetris

Tetris is one of the most popular puzzle games of all time. Stack falling blocks (tetrominoes) and complete horizontal lines to clear them. Plan your moves carefully and try to last as long as possible! This free online version brings the classic Tetris gameplay to your browser.

How to Play Tetris

  • ⬅️ ➡️ Use left/right arrows to move blocks
  • ⬆️ Up arrow to rotate blocks
  • ⬇️ Down arrow to soft drop
  • 🎮 Spacebar for hard drop

Game Features

  • 🎯 Classic Tetris gameplay
  • 🏆 Score points by clearing lines
  • 🎮 Simple and addictive mechanics
  • ⚡ Increasing difficulty levels
  • 🌟 Challenge yourself to beat high scores

Experience the timeless puzzle game that has captivated millions of players worldwide. Test your skills, strategy, and quick thinking in this classic block-stacking challenge. How long can you last as the speed increases?
